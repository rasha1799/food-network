Mediterranean diet

The Mediterranean diet is inspired by the eating patterns of countries like Italy, Spain, and Greece. A well-rounded Mediterranean diet should include plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, nuts, seeds, proteins, and healthy fats.

Plant-based diet

Plant-based diets encourage you to eat foods from plants, including fruits, vegetables, nuts, seeds, oils, and legumes.
